# Transcription & Captioning QA Checklist

## Accuracy
- Words accurately reflect spoken content
- No missing or added meaning
- Proper handling of negation

## Timing & Formatting
- Caption timing within acceptable thresholds
- Readable line length and segmentation
- Correct speaker attribution

## Non-Speech Elements
- Music, laughter, pauses, and sound effects labeled when required

## Severity Levels
- **Critical:** Meaning altered or wrong speaker
- **Major:** Timing or omission affecting comprehension
- **Minor:** Style or punctuation issues
